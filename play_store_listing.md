## Long Description

_(Copy everything between the triple backticks — 4000 character max)_

```
Captional is the fastest way to put your words directly on your photos.

No complicated editors. No layers. No learning curve. Just open the app, pick a photo, type your caption, and save — all in under 10 seconds.

────────────────────────
 WHY CAPTIONAL?
────────────────────────

When you post a photo on WhatsApp, Instagram, or Facebook, your caption lives separately from the image. The moment someone screenshots it or shares it, your words disappear.

Captional solves this by burning your caption directly into the photo — so your message travels with your image, always.

────────────────────────
 HOW IT WORKS
────────────────────────

1. Tap "Choose Photo" and pick any image from your gallery
2. Type your caption — up to 200 characters, emojis supported
3. Choose where it appears: Top, Center, or Bottom
4. Pick a style: Bold, Minimal, or Quote
5. Add a readability overlay: Gradient, Box, or None
6. Save to your gallery or share instantly

That's it. Your captioned photo is ready.

────────────────────────
 FEATURES
────────────────────────

✦ Caption positioning — place your text at the top, centre, or bottom of any photo

✦ Three caption styles — Bold for impact, Minimal for elegance, Quote for a literary feel

✦ Readability overlays — dark gradient, semi-transparent box, or no background at all

✦ Full emoji support — express yourself with any emoji in your caption

✦ Export as PNG or JPG — choose the format that works best for your platform

✦ Save to gallery — captioned photos go straight to your photo library

✦ Share anywhere — send directly to WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Messages, and more

✦ Recent photos — your last 6 picks are saved so you can come back to them anytime

✦ Works offline — no internet connection needed, everything happens on your device

✦ No watermark hidden in subscriptions — what you see is what you get

────────────────────────
 PERFECT FOR
────────────────────────

• WhatsApp status updates
• Instagram stories and posts
• Facebook photo captions
• Motivational quotes on photos
• Personal mood updates
• Sharing memories with context
• Students and young creators
• Anyone who posts daily

────────────────────────
 PRIVACY
────────────────────────

Captional processes everything locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any server. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

────────────────────────

Download Captional and give your photos a voice.
```
